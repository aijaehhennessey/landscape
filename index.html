No Country

Artist Description:

The original painting, "North Head from above Balmoral, Sydney Harbour" (1866) was done by English Australian watercolor painter Conrad Martens.

This is a very detailed painting but also represents a very colonial perspective that did not respect the land or its Indigenous inhabitants.

My re-interpretation of this painting focused on the basic shapes and colors to convey the base energy but incorporates the colors/ pattern of the Aboriginal flag.

This is meant to speak to the fact that Australia is stolen land and despite colonial processes, will always be native land with Indigenous peoples.
